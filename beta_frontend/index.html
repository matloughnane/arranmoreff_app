Arranmore Fast Ferry

Something nice about the Arranmore Fast Ferry.

Realt Na Maidne Teoranta Ferry Company (Bookings & Info 087 3171810) is located at Leabgarrow, Arranmore Island, Co Donegal and have been operating since June 2007. We run a daily schedule between Arranmore Island and Burtonport. Vessels are wheelchair friendly.

*Passengers with disabilities are advised to pre book your vehicle on ferry so that you can be given the designated parking area for easy access to onboard facilities.
