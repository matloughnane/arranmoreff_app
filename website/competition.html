Competition

We're very excited to announce our latest competition, we'll be giving away a massive €5000 to one of our lucky passengers at the end of the summer!

To be in with a chance of winning all you have to do is buy a return ticket from us and we'll enter your name in the draw. We've put posters up all over the county so be sure to help us spread the word on facebook and twitter - make sure you tag us too! We are running this draw in conjunction with our local radio station, Highland Radio. and will be announcing the winner on the Gary Gamble show on the 11th September 2015. Full terms and conditions for the drawer will be listed below.

Terms & Conditions of Draw

  1. All Passengers who purchase a full fare return ticket may enter draw.
  2. Drivers with a pass, who purchase a return ticket may enter draw.
  3. Groups of 12+, travelling on the same sailing and availing of the Group Discount may enter draw.
  4. Passengers who purchase return tickets online or via phone & paying by card, who receive 10% discount, may enter draw.
  5. Passengers who avail of the €45 family rate (2 adults & 2 chldren) may enter draw.
  6. Any other concessions apart from those listed above are not eligible to enter draw.
  7. Winner must be available to take phonecall on 11th September 2015.

Competition closes after last sailing on Thursday the 10th September 2015 and draw will take place live on the Gary Gamble show on Highland Radio on the 11th September 2015.
